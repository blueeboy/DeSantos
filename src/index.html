Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Santos family, De Santos Hotel began as a boutique establishment with just 15 rooms. Today, we've grown into a luxury destination while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and attention to detail.

Our philosophy is simple: create an environment where guests feel both pampered and at home. Every aspect of our hotel, from the architecture to the amenities, is designed with your comfort and pleasure in mind.

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional hospitality experience that combines luxury, comfort, and authentic local culture, creating memorable moments for every guest who walks through our doors.
